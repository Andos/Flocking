Flocking Object

Flocking object is an extension for Multimedia Fusion 2 that allows you to simulate the movement of 'flocks' of objects - like fish or birds.
This can be used for many visual effects or even gameplay mechanics.

The extension comes with many features to finely control how the boids in the flock moves and behaves to suit your needs.

Have a look in the properties section of this help file to see the different settings you can apply to your boids.
